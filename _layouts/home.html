Welcome to the IT Ethics Exploration

In a world increasingly driven by technology, understanding the ethical implications of IT practices is crucial. This website delves into the ethics of software development, highlighting the importance of ethical considerations in this rapidly evolving field.

Since the field of IT is expansive, our focus here is on the ethics of software development, examining how ethical principles apply to various aspects of the technology world.

About This Site

This website was designed as part of an academic assignment to explore IT Ethics. The layout and design are made possible through the use of Jekyll and GitHub Pages, demonstrating the practical application of these technologies in educational settings.

The template for the site was created by Professor Myeong Lee, adapted to suit the theme of IT Ethics.
